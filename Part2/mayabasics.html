MAYA ANIMATION BASICS

April 16th - May 26th, 2018
Every Thursdays 6:00 PM - 9:00pm

Autodesk Maya is a multifaceted software that is the industry standard for animation in the entertainment industry. Senior animator Mary Holmes who has worked for Electronic arts in such projects as FIFA and Madden. She will be leading a series of classes over a 6 week program outlining animation principles and processes.

  • No experience is required!
  • All our computers have full versions of Maya. Feel free to bring your own computer if you have it.
  • You will learn core principles such as lighting, modeling, texturing and animation.
  • You will learn and practice how to write external code in C# and Java.
  • At the end of this class you will have made your very own envronment.
