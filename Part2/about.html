ABOUT HIVE MIND

The art industry is a competitive field and most of the resources to build one’s skills are found online or through expensive institutions. Our goal with Hivemind is to establish a space where creators can meet one another and collaborate.

Those interested in getting into the entertainment industry can get feedback and learn from other’s strengths in a welcoming and motivating way.
