PIXAR DEMO

March 25, 2018 | 1:00PM - 4:30PM

Pixar animator, writer and director, Andrew Coats will be providing insight into the professional animation world through his own personal experiences.

Andrew has worked on celebrated films such as Brave and Inside Out. He will also be giving a short animation demo using Autodesk Maya highlighting some animation fundamentals and cornerstones to keep in mind when working on animation projects.

  • You will get a chance to learn and speak with a professional animator.
  • You will get to see an animator at work using popular 3D modeling program Maya.
  • You will have a chance to show off your own portfolio at the end of the demo.

Best of all, this event is free of charge!
