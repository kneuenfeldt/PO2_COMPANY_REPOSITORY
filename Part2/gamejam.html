HIVEMIND GAME JAM 2018

April 20-22, 2018 | 12:00 pm Friday - 12:00pm Sunday.

Hivemind is proud to host our first annual game jam. We invite artists, programmers, and designers to collaborate to create a completed video game using the unity game engine over a 2 day weekend.

Come with teams ahead of time or organize teams on site. Teams will compete and be judged by an experienced panel for a prize.

  • It will be a ton of fun. We promise!
  • You will get to put your knowledge to the test agaisnt other passionate people.
  • This is a great event to make new and bonding friendships

Best of all, this event is free of charge!
